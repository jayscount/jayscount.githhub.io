San Francisco, CA

Balmy Street Murals

    Where

  • Balmy Alley is located off of 24th Street in The Mission.
    It is parallel to Treat Ave and Harrison Street between 24th & 25th streets.

  • Take BART to 24th/Mission. Walk EAST (towards Capp Street or Carlos' Bar) for 5 1/2 blocks.

Balmy is a small alley with many pedestrians, please park nearby and walk through- it's a much better way to appreciate the murals.
(24th Street and small parts of the side streets are the only ones that are metered).
